Fun facts every blink should know

BLACKPINK means "pretty but not cute" - a concept of powerful femininity

The fandom name BLINK combines BLACKPINK and 'IN' meaning fans are connected

They were the first K-pop girl group to perform at Coachella (2019)

All 4 members are brand ambassadors for luxury houses (Dior, Chanel, YSL, Celine)

Members speak Korean, English, Japanese, Thai, and Chinese collectively

First K-pop group to have 4 music videos with 1B+ views each
